Get in Touch

At Dish & Spill we know the value of community. Our long tables focus on community dining and conversation with strangers, and we hope new friends. Too often members of our community are left with nobody to talk to resulting in loneliness and depression and so we decided to create a restaurant that would welcome in both solo diners and people happy to chat to someone knew. We'd love to hear from you if you would like to get involved in our upcoming community activities. Do you know any community groups that would benefit from a shared dining experience? Let us know! Or if you just want to ask us anything or leave any feedback we'd be delighted to hear from you.
